2 cups ice cubes, 1/4 cup lemon slices, 1/4 cup pineapple chunks, 1/4 cup tequila, 1/4 cup pineapple juice, 1/4 cup high alcohol content rum (recommended: Bacardi 151), Splash grenadine, 1/4 cup maraschino cherries, plus more for garnish
 	 In a pitcher, add all the ingredients and stir to combine.  Garnish with maraschino cherries, if using.  Pour into rocks glasses, garnish with maraschino cherries and serve.
