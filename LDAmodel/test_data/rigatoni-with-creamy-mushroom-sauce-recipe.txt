2 shallots, minced, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, 1/2 cup white wine, 1 cup (8 ounces) mascarpone cheese, at room temperature, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
 	 Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta and cook until tender but still firm to the bite, stirring occasionally, about 8 to 10 minutes.
