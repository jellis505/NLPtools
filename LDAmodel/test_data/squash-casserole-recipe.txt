Vegetable oil, 1 large onion, chopped, 4 tablespoons butter, 1 teaspoon House seasoning, recipe follows, 1 cup crushed butter crackers (recommended: Ritz), 1 cup salt, 1/4 cup black pepper , 1/4 cup garlic powder
 	 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 		Saute the squash in a little vegetable oil over medium-low heat until it has completely broken down, about 15 to 20 minutes. Line a colander with a clean tea towel. Place the cooked squash in the lined colander. Squeeze excess moisture from the squash. Set aside. 		In a medium size skillet, saute the onion in butter for 5 minutes. Remove from pan and mix all ingredients together except cracker crumbs. Pour mixture into a buttered casserole dish and top with cracker crumbs. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
