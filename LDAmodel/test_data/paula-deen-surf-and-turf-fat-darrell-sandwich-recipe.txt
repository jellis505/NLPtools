Salt and freshly ground black pepper, Garlic powder, 1 medium lobster tail, 4 tablespoons butter, 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 3/4 cup cream, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, Large club or hoagie rolls, split and toasted, Onion, sliced, optional
 	 Season the filet with salt and pepper, to taste, and garlic powder on both sides. Combine the remaining ingredients in a zip-top bag. Add the meat to the bag and marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours. 		Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. 		Transfer filet to a broiler pan and roast in the oven for 12 to 20 minutes, depending on desired doneness. Remove from oven and let rest before slicing.
