4 slices, country-style bread, Olive oil, Salt and pepper, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 4 beef fillet steaks, about 6 ounces, Salt and coarsely ground black pepper, 2 shallots, finely diced, 1 cup red wine, 2 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, 2 tablespoons olive oil, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 1/4 cups heavy cream, 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
 	 Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Place bread on a work surface and using a 3-inch cookie cutter, cut a round crouton from the center of each piece of bread.  Brush both sides of the croutons with olive oil and season with salt and pepper, to taste.  Place the croutons on a baking sheet and bake for 5 to 7 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
