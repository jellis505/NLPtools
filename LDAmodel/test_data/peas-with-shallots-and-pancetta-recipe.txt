2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 pound pancetta, cut into small dice, 3 shallots, halved and thinly sliced, Pinch red pepper flakes, 1 pound frozen peas, thawed
 	 Heat the oil in a large high sided saute pan over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until golden brown and the fat has rendered. Remove the pancetta to a plate lined with paper towels.  		  		Add the shallots and red pepper flakes to the pan and cook until soft. Add the peas and cook until warmed through. Transfer to a large bowl and top with the crisp pancetta.
