16 ounces chickpeas, 1 jalapeno, seeded, diced, 1 tablespoon chopped garlic, 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro leaves, 1 teaspoon paprika, 3 tablespoons chopped onion, 1 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper, 1 tablespoon chipotle pepper in adobo, Toasted pita bread, for serving
 	 Mix all ingredients, except olive oil and pita bread, in food processor until pureed, then slowly add in olive oil. 		Let set up for 1 hour prior to serving. 		Serve with toasted pita bread.
