1 bottle white wine (Spanish table wine), 3 ounces brandy, 3 ounces white peach puree (peel peaches, remove pit and puree in a blender with a small amount of water), Fresh peaches, oranges, and apples sliced
 	 Place all ingredients in a pitcher and stir to mix. Refrigerate at least 8 hours or up to 48 hours. Serve over ice.
