1 head iceburg lettuce, washed, patted dry, and torn into pieces, 1/2 cup diced celery, 1 cup frozen green peas, thawed, uncooked, 3/4 cup raisins, 3/4 cup chopped nuts (pecans, walnuts, or salted peanuts), 1 cup grated Cheddar, 3/4 cup chopped green onions, green part only, 10 to 12 slices bacon, cooked until crisp, chopped, , 2 cups mayonnaise, 1/4 cup sugar
 	 In a large rectangular dish, layer salad ingredients in the order listed, stopping after the nuts. Mix dressing ingredients and let stand for 5 minutes. Spread dressing over entire top of salad, covering it completely. Sprinkle cheese, green onions, and bacon over salad. Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving.
