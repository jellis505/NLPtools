1 teaspoon House Seasoning (garlic powder, black pepper, and salt mix), 1 medium onion, chopped, 1 cup grated Cheddar, 1 cup crushed crackers (recommended: Ritz), 1 teaspoon seasoned salt (recommended: Lawry's), 2 slices white bread, Sauce, recipe follows, 1 (10 3/4-ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, 1 cup milk, 1 1/2 cups grated Cheddar, Chopped onions, optional
 	 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  		  		Mix all ingredients together, except the bread slices and Sauce. Shape into a loaf. Line a 1 1/2-quart loaf pan with the bread slices. Place meatloaf on top. Bake loaf for 45 minutes. The bread absorbs the grease and should be discarded after loaf is removed from oven.
