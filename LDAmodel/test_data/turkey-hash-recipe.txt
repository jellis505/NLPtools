2 red onions, diced, 2 jalapenos, diced, and seeded, 2 pounds ground turkey breast, 1 teaspoon salt, 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, 1/2 lime
 	 In medium saute pan, add 2 ounces olive oil, onions, peppers and saute until translucent. Add turkey and brown on all sides. Season with salt and pepper.  		  		In a separate pan, add 2 ounces of oil, and fry potatoes in oil until crispy. On a serving plate layer the turkey mixture on top of the crispy potatoes.   		  		Top with diced roma tomatoes and fresh squeezed lime juice.
