1/2 pound Israeli couscous, Salt, 12 spears asparagus, grilled and cut into 1/4-inch pieces, 1 zucchini, halved, grilled and cut into 1-inch pieces, 1 yellow squash, halved, grilled and cut into 1-inch pieces, 2 large red peppers, grilled, peeled and diced into bite-size pieces, 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves, Freshly ground black pepper, Lemon-Balsamic Vinaigrette, recipe follows, 1 small shallot, minced, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon lemon zest, 3 tablespoons aged balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
 	 Heat large saute pan on grates of the grill over medium heat. Add couscous and toast until lightly golden brown.  		   		Bring 6 cups of water to a boil over high heat, add 1 tablespoon salt and toasted couscous and cook until al dente. Drain well and place in a large bowl. Add grilled vegetables, olives, basil, and vinaigrette and toss until combined; season with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before serving or cover and refrigerate.
