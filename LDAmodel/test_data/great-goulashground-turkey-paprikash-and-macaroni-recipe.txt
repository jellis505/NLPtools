1/2 pound elbow macaroni, Salt, 1 tablespoon butter , 1 package, 1 1/3 pounds average weight, ground lean white turkey, 1 medium onion, chopped, 1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped, 2 tablespoons sweet paprika, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon dried marjoram , Black pepper, 1 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons chopped dill
 	 Bring a pot of water to boil for pasta. When it comes to a boil, add pasta and salt to season the cooking water. Cook pasta 6 minutes to al dente. 		While water comes to a boil and pasta cooks, heat a deep skillet over medium high heat. Add extra-virgin olive oil then butter then ground meat. Break up meat and crumble, 2 to 3 minutes. Add garlic, onions, red bell peppers and seasonings to the turkey. Cook 5 or 6 minutes then add chicken stock and sour cream to the pan. Bring to a bubble and reduce heat to low. Add cooked pasta and stir. Let pasta absorb some sauce, a minute or so. Adjust seasonings and serve. Garnish the prepared dish with chopped parsley and dill.
