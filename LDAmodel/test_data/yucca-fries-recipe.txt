1 tablespoon fine sea salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 1 teaspoon paprika, 2 pounds fresh yucca, 1 lime zested
 	 In medium Dutch oven heat oil to 350 degrees F. 		Place all spices in mixing bowl, and combine thoroughly. 		Peel yucca tubes with heavy-duty vegetable peeler until bark is completely removed. Cut yucca into strips: 1/4-inch wide, 4 inches long. Add yucca into oil and fry until golden brown. With a slotted spoon remove the yucca from the oil and drain on paper towels. 		Season hot yucca fries with spice mixture and lime zest and serve on a warm paper-towel-lined platter.
