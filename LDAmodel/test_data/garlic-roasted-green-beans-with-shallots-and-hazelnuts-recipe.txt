1 pound green beans, trimmed, 1 shallot, thinly sliced, Salt and freshly ground pepper, 1/4 cup hazelnuts, toasted and chopped
 	 Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. 		Toss together green beans, shallots, garlic, oil and salt and pepper in a medium roasting pan. Roast in the oven until the beans are cooked through and light golden brown. Remove from the oven and stir in the lemon zest and hazelnuts. Transfer to a platter and serve.
